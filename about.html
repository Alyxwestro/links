ABOUT WORK = PLAY

Music is the element that connects "WORK" and "PLAY" to me. As if you enjoy different genres of music on your playlist, the concept of a website is a music player where it contains all different kinds of content, from food to design work (play to work). You can change a channel to enjoy different playlists on a cozy night.
This website is for a graduate project in the Typography & Interaction class (Coding), Communication Design program at Parsons School of Design | The New School.
